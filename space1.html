   The moon🌕


The moon is the natural satellite of the earth, visible (chiefly at night) by reflected light from the sun.🌙








The first moon landing, the Apollo 11 mission, occurred on July 20, 1969. NASA The astronauts Neil Armstrong and Buzz Aldrin were the first humans to walk on the Moon.















    Fun facts❓❓❓


    - 👂 Space is silent: There's no air in space, so sound waves can't travel, making it a silent vacuum.


    - 🦶 Footprints on the Moon are permanent: The footprints left by astronauts on the Moon will remain visible for millions of years due to the absence of wind and rain.


    - 🏖 Stars outnumber sand grains: There are more stars in the universe than grains of sand on all the beaches on Earth.


    - ⭐ The Sun is a star: The Sun is a giant star, not a planet.


    - 🥵 The hottest planet isn't the closest: Venus is the hottest planet in our solar system, despite being further from the Sun than Mercury.
